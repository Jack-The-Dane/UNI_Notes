Obs $X_1, X_2, X_3 .., X_n$ iid (Independent, identically distributed), does not have to be normally distributed, just distributed in the same way.
$$\bar X = {1 \over n} \sum_{j=1}^{n}{x_j} \sim(approx) N_p(\mu, {\Sigma \over n})$$
Så middelværdien vil være ca normalfordelt, selvom det originale datasæt ikke er.
